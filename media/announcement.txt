Do you like solving logic puzzles? Intrigued by stories of knights and knaves? Curious what Inspector Craig of Scotland Yard will encounter in an enchanted forest? The CS club has just the book for you! Raymond Smullyan's To Mock A Mockingbird is a computer science classic. Through fun logic puzzles and stories about birds, we will learn the fundamentals of combinatory logic, culminating in Godel's first incompleteness theorem!
